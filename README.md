# Financial-Health-Performance-Dashboard
## FY2025 Financial Analysis Case Study

---
##  Project Objective  
The objective of this project was to develop a comprehensive financial dashboard to monitor the health of a major hospitality entity. By synthesizing data from the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement (2024–2025), the project identifies key trends in revenue growth, margin compression, and liquidity management.


##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue: $3.48 million (17.2% year-over-year growth)  
- Gross Margin: 24.3% (positive trend)  
- Net Profit: $1.14 million (-189,442)    
- Operating Margin:35.3% (-14.3%)
- Net Margin: 32.9% (-13.4%)
- Free CashFlow: $990,775 (positive trend) 
- Working Capital:$644,809 (positive trend) 
- Cashflow From Operation:$769,132 (positive trend)

### How does the dashboard visualize revenue performance?  

The dashboard highlights a 17.2% increase in sales, rising from $2.88 million to $3.48 million. This indicates successful market expansion. Gross Margin also improved, confirming strong core service profitability.


---

### Why is the bottom line flagged as critical despite record sales?  
Net Margin declined by 13.4%. The dashboard shows that administrative and general overhead costs increased faster than revenue, creating an efficiency gap that reduced overall profitability.

---

### What does the dashboard show regarding financial stability?   
Liquidity is strong. Working Capital increased to $644,809, and cash reserves exceeded $1 million. The dashboard confirms that the entity is financially stable and well-positioned for future investments.
* **Cash Flow from Operations ($769,132)** and **Free Cash Flow ($990,775)** are both on a positive trend.
* **Working Capital ($644,809)** reflects a strong safety net to cover short-term debts.

---

### What is the primary tactical recommendation based on the data?    
Reducing internal overhead costs is the most effective way to improve profit margins and increase shareholder value.

---

## Technical Implementation  

- Data Integration:  
  Combined data from multiple financial statements into a unified model  

- Variance Analysis:  
  Implemented calculations to detect and flag performance changes based on year-over-year metrics  

- Liquidity Tracking:  
  Built formulas to monitor Working Capital and Free Cash Flow dynamically  

---

## Final Conclusion  

The analysis shows that the firm is in a strong growth phase but is experiencing rising operational costs. The dashboard provides clear visibility into performance
